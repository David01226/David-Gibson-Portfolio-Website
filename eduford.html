Project Overview

This project was a coding challenge I found online and was the first full website I ever coded. It allowed me to practice essential positioning methods such as, flexbox and introduced me to styling practices such as, on hover and pseudoelements. It was also my first experience with PHP. I later revisited this whilst building my portfolio and is used in the form on the contact page.
